ADAMS Membership

Membership Classifications & Enrollment Criteria

The Association offers two distinct categories of membership. Please review the eligibility requirements and fee structures outlined below to determine the appropriate registration path.

Choose membership & sign up Already have an account?

1. Lifetime Membership

Designed for established medical professionals seeking permanent affiliation with the Association.

Eligibility
Open to registered medical practitioners. Applicants must hold a valid registration with any State Medical Council (SMC), the Medical Council of India (MCI), or the National Medical Commission (NMC).
Validity
Permanent (Lifetime).
Enrollment Fee
A one-time induction fee of ₹2,500.
Maintenance
Members are subject to annual Maintenance Charges (AMC). The AMC rate is reviewed periodically and is subject to change based on Association directives.

2. Adhoc Membership

A provisional membership tier structured for students and medical graduates in transition.

Eligibility
This category is specifically reserved for:
  • Foreign Medical Students & FMG Candidates (Current/Passed).
  • Interns (Domestic and International).
  • Clerkship Candidates.
Validity
Valid for the duration of one Financial Year. Membership must be renewed annually to remain active.
Enrollment Fee
₹2,000 for the 2026–2027 Financial Year.
Maintenance
Adhoc members are exempt from Annual Maintenance Charges.

Terms & Application Protocol

  • Pre-Application Inquiry: To ensure all allotment criteria are fulfilled, all prospective applicants are required to contact the Association at helpdeskadams@gmail.com prior to submitting their application or payment.
  • Scrutiny & Approval: All submitted applications shall undergo a formal vetting process by the Executive Committee (EC).
  • Processing Timeline: Please allow a minimum of one month for the processing and scrutiny of membership applications.
  • Confirmation: Upon successful approval by the EC, applicants will receive an official confirmation of their membership status directly from the Association.
  • Forfeiture Policy: Please be advised that in the event an application is rejected during the scrutiny process, the application fee shall be forfeited and is non-refundable.

All applications are subject to verification of credentials. Please ensure all registration documents are current at the time of submission.
