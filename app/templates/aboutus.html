TVM/TC/16/2024

ADAMS is a registered non-profit association dedicated to serving doctors and medical students who are abroad-trained Indian nationals. We operate as part of the All Kerala Foreign Medical Association (AKFMA), with the aim of bringing together all Foreign Medical Graduates.

"The formation of ADAMS was driven by the need for an organization that advocates for the rights of every foreign medico in the country, including doctors and medical students."
