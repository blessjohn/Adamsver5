About ADAMS

Association of Doctors and Medical Students

Association of Doctors and Medical Students

Registration No: TVM/TC/16/2024

ADAMS is a registered non-profit association dedicated to supporting Indian doctors and medical students who have pursued medical education abroad.

We function under the All Kerala Foreign Medical Association (AKFMA) with the aim of uniting all Foreign Medical Graduates and advocating for their professional rights, welfare, and career progression.

“ADAMS was formed to ensure that every foreign medical graduate in India has a strong, unified voice.”
— Dr. Renjith C R, Founder
